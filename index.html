The EQAITE app

The EQAITE app is a web application that allows you to evaluate AI tools  against the eqAIte framework and display the results on a radar map.  It is designed to help you understand the capabilities and limitations  of AI tools and make informed decisions about their use.  The EQAITE app is provided on an “as is” and “as available” basis  without any warranties of any kind.

 On hitting submit, you are permitting us to save your responses for  research purposes. We will never share your personally  identifiable information with third parties.  We may aggregate your anonymised responses with those of other  users and share the aggregated data.

 You can read more about our privacy policy and terms of use here: EQAITE Terms of Use and Privacy Policy. If you have any questions or concerns, please contact us: info (at) eqaite.org
